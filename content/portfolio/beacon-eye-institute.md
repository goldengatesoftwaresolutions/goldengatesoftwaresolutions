Beacon Eye Institute is a full-featured marketing and information website built for an ophthalmology practice. The site gives patients easy access to information about the practice, its physicians, and the wide range of eye-care services offered.

## Project Overview

Golden Gate Software Solutions designed and developed a 40-page static site organized across 7 distinct sections, giving the practice a professional online presence that is fast, accessible, and easy to maintain.

## Key Features

- **40 pages** of patient-focused content covering conditions, treatments, and staff
- **7 content sections** including Services, Conditions, Physicians, Patient Resources, Locations, Blog, and Contact
- Clean, accessible design optimized for patients of all ages
- Fully responsive layout for mobile, tablet, and desktop
- Deployed as a high-performance static site with zero server overhead

## Technology

Built with Hugo and deployed as a static site, ensuring fast load times, strong security, and simple long-term maintenance.
