Jameson PLLC is a technology-focused law practice based in Arizona. Golden Gate Software Solutions built a polished, professional website that reflects the firm's expertise at the intersection of law and technology.

## Project Overview

The site presents the firm's practice areas, attorney profiles, and contact information in a clean, trustworthy design suited to a modern legal practice. The focus on technology law required a site that felt both professional and forward-thinking.

## Key Features

- Clear presentation of technology-law practice areas
- Attorney profiles and firm background
- Streamlined contact experience for prospective clients
- Fully responsive design for all screen sizes
- Deployed as a fast, secure static site

## Technology

Built with Hugo and deployed as a static site, providing the firm with a low-maintenance, high-performance web presence.
