Golden Gate Software Solutions is a San Francisco-based software development studio. Founded in 2023, we aim to bring innovative, scalable software development to your startup or small business.

## Our Mission

Jackson Galan founded Golden Gate Software Solutions to fill the technology gap many small businesses experience. Owners and operators often struggle to realize their vision, given the complexity of software systems in the market. What products from WordPress to AI site generators offers may seem like a magic bullet, in reality, developers need a solid foundation in application architecture, and an in-depth understanding of core technologies to bring an idea to production.

Golden Gate Software Soltions brings 7+ years of experience at all levels of development, from toy projects and prototypes, to simple, elegant site design, to production-grade scalable systems implementation. Jackson leverages bleeding-edge AI for rapid development, undergirded by tried-and-true engineering fundamentals.
